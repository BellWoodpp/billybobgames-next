How to play: Toggle sound in the top-right corner, then press Play to begin. Use your mouse to swing the hammer and hit every mole before it hides. Build streaks to boost your score, clear each wave before the timer drains, and tap Pause whenever you need a breather.
       

Tips & Controls

  • Swap between waves by clearing every mole before the timer bar empties.
  • Click and hold to swing the hammer faster; release to trigger a strike.
  • Use the pause button in the top-right corner whenever you need a quick break.
  • Keep combos alive to earn bonus points and unlock tougher stages.
